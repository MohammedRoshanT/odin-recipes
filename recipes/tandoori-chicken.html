Tandoori Chicken

Home

Description

Tandoori Chicken is a flavorful Indian dish made by marinating chicken in spiced yogurt and grilling it until tender and smoky. Traditionally cooked in a clay oven (tandoor), it gets its signature taste from spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The result is a juicy, slightly charred chicken with rich, aromatic flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, oil, garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, garam masala, paprika, and salt to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to a high temperature (around 200°C/400°F).
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on the grill or in a baking dish.
  5. Cook for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char.
  6. Serve hot with naan bread or rice and enjoy!
